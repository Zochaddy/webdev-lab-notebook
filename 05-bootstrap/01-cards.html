Chrome Dino Game

The Chrome Dino Game was released in 2014 by Google. It appears when users lose internet connection in the Chrome browser.

Players control a small dinosaur that runs endlessly, jumping over obstacles like cacti and dodging flying enemies. It has become an iconic offline pastime.

Untitled Goose Game

Untitled Goose Game was released in 2019 by House House. You play as a mischievous goose causing chaos in a quiet English village.

The game is a stealth puzzle game where you complete tasks like stealing items and honking at unsuspecting villagers. It became a viral sensation.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird was released in 2013 by Dong Nguyen. The player taps the screen to keep a bird flying between pipes without hitting them.

Despite its simple design, the game became one of the most downloaded apps in the world before its creator removed it from app stores in 2014.
